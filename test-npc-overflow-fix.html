Thornwick Isle

  • Commands underwater horrors that patrol the bay

  • Knows the location of ancient artifacts buried beneath the bay
  • Can transform into a massive tentacled beast when threatened

Motivations:

  • Maintain absolute control over Scrimshaw Bay and its inhabitants
  • Spread corruption throughout the region to weaken reality
  • Eliminate any threats to his dominion

Abilities:

  • Tentacle transformation and combat
  • Command over sea creatures and corrupted beings
  • Psychic domination and mind control
  • Immortality through his pact with Agog
  • Weather manipulation over the bay

Dr. Helena Voss

Millhaven Clinic
Town Doctor & Researcher
Brilliant physician who arrived in Millhaven three years ago. She has dedicated herself to studying the strange ailments affecting the townspeople and is the closest to understanding the true source of the corruption.

Secrets:

  • Has documented evidence of the parasite infections
  • Secretly performs autopsies on transformed citizens
  • Maintains a hidden laboratory in the clinic basement
  • Has developed a partial resistance serum
  • Knows the transformation process is accelerating

Motivations:

  • Find a cure for the mysterious parasitic illness
  • Save as many townspeople as possible
  • Understand the scientific basis of the corruption
  • Expose the truth about what's happening to the bay
  • Protect her research from Lord Scrimm's agents

Abilities:

  • Advanced medical knowledge and surgical skills
  • Research and scientific analysis
  • Partial resistance to corruption
  • Alchemy and chemical synthesis
  • Network of informants among the townspeople

Captain Tobias Grimm

Millhaven Harbor

Motivations:

  • Prevent others from suffering the same fate as his crew
  • Find a way to escape the bay with his family
  • Preserve the knowledge of the old ways

Abilities:

  • Combat experience with sea monsters

  • Survival skills in harsh maritime conditions
